# Linux: Origin Story (History & Philosophy)

- **Unix (1970s):** Powerful but expensive and closed.  
- **Linux (1991):** Created by Linus Torvalds as a free, Unix-like OS for personal computers.  

- **Open Source & Free:**  
  - Licensed under **GPL**.  
  - "Free as in Freedom" → anyone can view, modify, and share the code.  

- **GNU Project:**  
  - Created by **Richard Stallman**.  
  - Provides essential tools (compiler, shell, utilities).  
  - Combined with Linux kernel → **GNU/Linux** (complete OS).
